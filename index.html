Game Rules

  • Goal: Score as many points as possible by clearing lines.

  • Scoring: 1 point is awarded for every row cleared.

  • Clearing lines: After 2 lines are cleared, the difficulty increases

  • Game Over: The game ends when the blocks reach the top of the screen.

Controls

  • 'A' button - Move left

  • 'D' button - Move right

  • 'S' button - Soft drop

  • 'R' button - Rotate clockwise

  • 'H' button - Hold
